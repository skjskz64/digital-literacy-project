## Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The aim of this project is to develop essential digital skills required in today’s academic and professional environment. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I worked on various tasks that focused on creating awareness about digital literacy, building a professional online presence, exploring useful digital platforms, practising formal communication, and understanding cyber safety.

The project is divided into five tasks, each covering a different aspect of digital literacy and helping in developing practical knowledge and responsible digital behavior.

---

## Tasks Summary

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
Created an infographic using Canva to explain key concepts such as digital literacy, safe internet practices,useful digital tools for students, and email etiquette in a simple and visually appealing way.

---

### Task 2: Digital Portfolio
Built and updated profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish a professional digital presence and understand their importance for future opportunities.

---

### Task 3: Platforms Exploration
Completed a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to understand collaboration tools and data collection methods.

🔗 *Google Form Link:* https://forms.gle/4ibQkqgKQKbQR5BGA

---

### Task 4: Email Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails (requesting assignment extension and applying for an internship) and created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist.

---

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Researched UPI fraud and created a case study along with a prevention checklist to spread awareness about online safety and responsible digital practices.
