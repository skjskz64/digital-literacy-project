SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS FOR
COLLEGE STUDENTS

DO’S:
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your post
  and comments.
- Think before posting and always ensure your content 
   reflects a positive digital identity.
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal 
   information.
- Share accurate and verified information to avoid spreading 
  misinformation.
- Be respectful towards others’ opinions and engage in 
  healthy discussions.


 DON’TS:
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Avoid sharing personal details like phone number, address, 
  or passwords.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling.
- Avoid posting content that can harm your academic or
  professional reputation.
- Do not click on or share suspicious links or unverified 
  information.
